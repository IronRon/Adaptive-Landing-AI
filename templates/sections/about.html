About SparkleWash

Founded in 2018, SparkleWash started with a single bay and a big dream: make professional-grade car care accessible to everyone. Today we operate 12 locations and serve over 10,000 members every month.

Our team of trained technicians uses eco-friendly, biodegradable products and reclaimed water systems to minimise our environmental footprint while delivering a spotless finish every time.

We believe in transparency — no hidden fees, no upsells, just honest service. That's why every membership comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee and the freedom to cancel anytime.

Whether you drive a daily commuter or a weekend sports car, SparkleWash treats every vehicle like it's our own. Come see the difference — your car will thank you.
