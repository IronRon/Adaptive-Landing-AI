Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your membership.

How does the unlimited wash membership work?

Sign up for a plan, attach your membership tag to your windshield, and drive through any SparkleWash location as often as you like. There are no per-wash charges and no limits on visits.

Can I cancel at any time?

Absolutely. There are no contracts or cancellation fees. You can cancel online or in-store, and your membership stays active until the end of the current billing cycle.

What's included in each plan?

Basic covers unlimited exterior washes and vacuums. Plus adds bi-weekly interior cleaning, tyre shine, and Rain-X. Premium adds monthly full details, quarterly ceramic coating, and priority-lane access.

How am I billed?

Monthly plans are billed on the same calendar date you signed up. Annual plans are billed once upfront at a 20 % discount. We accept all major credit cards and Apple Pay.

Can I use my membership at any location?

Yes! Your membership is valid at all SparkleWash locations nationwide. Just scan your windshield tag and you're good to go.

What if it rains right after my wash?

With unlimited washes, just come back! No charge, no hassle. That's the beauty of a membership — wash as often as the weather demands.

Do you offer a satisfaction guarantee?

Yes — if you're not 100 % happy with a wash, let us know within 24 hours and we'll re-wash your vehicle for free, no questions asked.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Of course. You can switch plans at any time from your online account. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.
